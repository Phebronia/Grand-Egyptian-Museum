Family Favourite

Children's Museum

Calling all young explorers for an exciting ancient Egyptian adventure at the GEM Children's Museum.

This dedicated space is designed to spark curiosity and imagination through hands-on learning experiences tailored specifically for young minds. Children can discover the wonders of ancient Egyptian civilisation in a fun, engaging, and interactive environment.

Opening Hours
  • Sunday to Thursday: 1:00 PM – 5:00 PM
  • Friday & Saturday: 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM
Important Information
  • Hands-on activities designed for children aged 6–12
  • Interactive zones introducing ancient Egypt through play
  • An interactive guidebook is available to enhance the experience
  • Children must be accompanied by an adult at all times
Why Visit
  • Fun and educational experience for kids
  • Encourages creativity and curiosity
  • Safe and engaging environment
  • Perfect for families

Most Popular

GEM Discovery Challenge

Make your visit to the Grand Egyptian Museum unforgettable by joining the GEM Discovery Challenge with your friends and family.

Explore a selection of fascinating artifacts through a fun and interactive experience. Follow clues, solve puzzles, and uncover the hidden treasure key. Winners of the challenge will receive a special souvenir to remember their adventure.

Game Schedule
  • The game is offered daily at 12 PM, 2 PM, 4 PM
  • Additional slot: 6:00 PM (Wednesdays & Saturdays)
Important Information
  • Each participant must have their own ticket
  • The challenge can take up to 1 hour
  • Suitable for all ages
  • Children under 12 must be accompanied by someone aged 16+ or an adult
  • A smartphone is required to participate
Why You'll Love It
  • Fun and interactive experience
  • Perfect for friends and families
  • Discover ancient Egyptian artifacts in an exciting way
  • Chance to win a souvenir
